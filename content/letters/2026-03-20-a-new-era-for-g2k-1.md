G2K stands at a crossroads.

For years we have been the most energetic youth organisation in Jamaican politics. We have produced speakers, debaters, chapter presidents, and community leaders who went on to reshape their constituencies. That legacy is real, and I am proud of every part of it.

But legacy alone does not win the next decade. What wins the next decade is structure.

What I am offering is not a rebrand. It is a rebuild.

Three pillars guide this campaign: Chapter and Member Development, National Policy and Thought Leadership, and Sustainable Financing. Each one was chosen because of a gap I have seen up close, in every chapter I have visited and every forum I have sat in.

We will professionalise how members are trained and recognised. We will create a genuine policy voice that government cannot ignore. And we will build financial independence so that no G2K administration ever again has to wait on someone else to fund its ambitions.

This is not a symbolic campaign. The G2K Presidency is an operational post, and I intend to operate it accordingly.

I ask for your confidence. Not because of what I have done, but because of what we will build together.

**Newton Harris**
Candidate for G2K President, 2026
